Welcome to Club Payne

Why Join Club Payne?

Club Payne is more than a club; it's a family commitment to honor our shared history and preserve the Lone Mountain House for future generations. It's our way to stay connected to our ancestors and each other.

Who Can Join?

If you're a descendant of Byrd and A Payne, you're already part of the family! By joining Club Payne, you can have a say in how we care for our cherished Lone Mountain House.

How Do I Participate?

It's simple! Your yearly donation of $25 or more makes you an active member. And the more you donate, the bigger your voice in any voting matters that may arise.

Your Contributions Matter

Your donations help us preserve the Lone Mountain House. But they also give you a stake in its future, including any potential sales proceeds. This is a unique way to invest in our family's legacy.

Stay Connected

Your membership keeps you in the loop about family gatherings, updates on the Lone Mountain House, and any important votes. It's the best way to stay connected to the family and our shared history.

Join Club Payne today and be a part of something bigger!
